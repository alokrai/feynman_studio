Who We Are

feynman studio is a digital agency based in Paris and London run by software engineers who care about building great products.

We seek to understand, design, and build innovative mobile and web apps, delightful websites, and beautiful e-commerce stores using our 4D development model.

Define

Our initial consultation consists of at least two meetings to understand and define your problem, pain points, and use cases. We also require mandatory meetings with end-users. Thereafter, we present the problem statement: a clear articulation of your needs. The problem statement is reviewed and revised by all parties, and the problem statement remains our North Star during entire development cycle.

Design

We design our solution fitting your needs. This could be wireframe, a prototype, or a simplified functioning version of the final product. The emphasis is on defining the solution quickly and refining it iteratively with user feedback.

Develop

Our favorite part. Here, we put our heads down and build a well- documented, thoroughly tested product for use. We present our product, match with with our problem statement, and make any changes necessary to solve our initial problem. We prefer to keep the stack simple, using popular technologies: Ruby on Rails, ReactJS, and NodeJS.

Deploy

We take into account the operational capabilities of our client to recommend a deployment strategy and solution, and train client's team to effectively use our solutions. We also offer maintenance and support.
